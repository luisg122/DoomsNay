#VisualizeCovid

Scope of Pandemics

This pandemic has placed us in very difficult moments. We still remain unsure of the true scope that this current pandemic has reached or may yet reach. But this uncertainty can be dealt with. By visualizing the extent of this pandemic, we can see areas with communities that were most affected by the virus with the help of tweets.


#GetInformed

Importance of good info

Information should be accessible to anyone, all day, every day, especially during pandemics. Expert advice from trusted sources could be life-saving and eye-opening. So follow the latest on what experts are saying in their tweets.
